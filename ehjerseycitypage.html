JERSEY CITY: THE "SIXTH BOROUGH OF NEW YORK"?

Introduction

The shadow of the Empire State Building extends westward in the morning skyacross the Hudson River. Underneath this shadow lies the city of Jersey City. Jersey City's history is closely entwined with that of New York City. Both were originally settled by the Dutch before the arrival of the British in 1664-1665.

Today, Jersey City is home to many ex-New Yorkers who left New York City in search of more business, professional and educational opportunities, as well as more living space at half the cost in either property values or rent, not to mention lower state/city taxes. The number of ex-New Yorkers is evidenced just by the license plates on their cars parked throughout Jersey City's sidewalks, parking lots and garages.
